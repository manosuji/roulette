the day flickers

stop-motion on bedroom ceiling

so that  you  remember  the

motionless  wet  of rooms so sweet and still

the  flush  of breath you know will come back to you


eyes  soft like nectarine

dimpled in the firefly glow of summer

(curling) inward


call me  starling 

blood thrumming wingbeat pulse all aflutter
